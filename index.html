Welcome to Takomon

A dynamic NFT collection on the Cardano blockchain, featuring handmade pixel art with unique, 1-of-1 collectibles that evolve through three stages - egg, baby, and grown form.

Roadmap

Stage 1: NFT Collection Launch

Our unique Takomon collection will be launched on Cardano, each one evolving from an egg to a fully grown digital octopus.

Stage 2: Web3 Tamagotchi Game

Interact with your Takomons in a Web3 Tamagotchi-style game, where you can nurture and play with your tentacled friends.

Stage 3: Token Launch

The Takomon ecosystem will introduce its own crypto token, enabling transactions and providing in-game accessories for the Tamagotchi game.

"Tako" (タコ) comes from the Japanese word for octopus. We’ve embraced this name to honor the spirit of the octopus, a creature of intelligence, creativity, and mystery.
